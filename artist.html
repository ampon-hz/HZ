THE ARTIST




Hazel is a 21 year old artist.
Assertive and goal-oriented, she applies both rationality and creativity to her works.


To create is to forget oneself. LUPIN was created to steal the intangible thoughts and ghostlike delusions from my mind and set them free. Lingering thoughts are calmed by visualizations. Raging feelings from the past and the restless mind are eased. That is the reason I created LUPIN, to scatter my racing thoughts towards the past and the future.





THE MIND OF LUPIN

LUPIN is a collection of artworks done and arranged by the artist, Hazel Ampon. Started in 2020, LUPIN combines a surrealistic approach with aesthetics, while improving the bonds between artist and viewer.
